Refund Policy

Introduction

This Refund Policy outlines the terms and conditions for refunds related to the purchase of Argo Sales Tracker license keys. We strive to provide high-quality software that meets your needs, but we understand that sometimes a purchase may not work out as expected.

License Key Purchases

Eligibility for Refunds

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for purchases of Argo Sales Tracker license keys, subject to the following conditions:

  1. The refund request must be made within 14 days of the original purchase date.
  2. You must provide a valid reason for requesting a refund.
  3. The license key must not have been used extensively (as determined by our system logs).

Ineligible for Refunds

Refunds will not be provided in the following situations:

  1. More than 14 days have passed since the original purchase date.
  2. The license key has been used extensively.
  3. The license key was purchased as part of a special promotion or discount specifically marked as non-refundable.
  4. The license key was obtained through fraudulent means.
  5. The license key was revoked due to violation of our Terms of Service.

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact our support team with the following information:

  1. Your name and email address used for the purchase.
  2. Order number or transaction ID.
  3. Date of purchase.
  4. Reason for requesting a refund.

You can contact our support team:

  • By email: contact@argorobots.com
  • Through the contact form on our website

Refund Processing

Once your refund request has been approved:

  1. We will deactivate your license key.
  2. The refund will be processed back to the original payment method used for the purchase.
  3. Refunds typically take 5-10 business days to appear on your statement, depending on your payment provider.

Changed Mind/Cancellations

If you change your mind about your purchase or ordered the wrong type of license by mistake, please contact us as soon as possible. We will try to accommodate your request if it falls within our refund eligibility period.

Technical Issues

If you are experiencing technical issues with Argo Sales Tracker, we encourage you to:

  1. Review our Documentation for troubleshooting guides and frequently asked questions
  2. Contact our technical support team through our Contact Page or by email

Many issues can be resolved with proper guidance, and we are committed to helping you get the most out of our software.

If technical issues cannot be resolved after reasonable attempts by our support team, we may offer a refund at our discretion.

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Refund Policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting to our website. It is your responsibility to review this Refund Policy periodically for changes.
