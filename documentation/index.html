Argo Sales Tracker Documentation

Welcome to the Argo Sales Tracker documentation. This guide will help you get started and make the most of our software.

System Requirements

  • Windows 10 or newer
  • 4GB RAM minimum
  • 1GB free disk space
  • 1280*720 screen resolution or higher

Installation Guide

  1. Download the installer from our website here
  2. Run the installer file (ArgoSalesTracker-Setup.exe)
  3. Follow the installation wizard instructions
  4. Launch Argo Sales Tracker from your desktop or start menu
Note: Windows may display a security warning. This is normal for new applications. Click "More info" and then "Run anyway" to proceed.

Quick Start Tutorial

  1. Choose your default currency and create your first company
  2. Add accountants and companies you will be working with
  3. Set up categories to organize your products
  4. Add your initial products
  5. Add purchases and sales

Product Management

Creating Categories to organize your products

  1. Go to "Manage Categories" in the top menu
  2. Choose whether the category is for purchases or sales
  3. Enter the category name
  4. Click "Add Category"

Adding Products

  1. Go to "Manage Products" in the top menu
  2. Select whether the product is for purchases or sales
  3. Enter the product ID and name
  4. Select a category to keep things organized
  5. Enter country and company of origin
  6. Click "Add Product"

Let's say you run a t-shirt store. Here's how you might set up one category with three products:

Category: Men's T-Shirts
Product ID Product Name Country Company
TS001 Black T-Shirt United States Cotton Mills Ltd
TS002 White Linen T-Shirt Italy FabricCo
TS003 Black T-Shirt China NotASweatShopCo
Important: Free version users are limited to 10 products. Upgrade to the paid version here for unlimited products.

Adding Purchases and Sales

  1. Click "Add Purchase" or "Add Sale" in the top menu
  2. Enter the order number and select your name from the accountants list
  3. Select the product from the dropdown (must be added in Product Management first)
  4. Enter the quantity and price per unit
  5. Add shipping costs, taxes, and any other fees
  6. Optionally attach a receipt
  7. Click "Add" to save
Tip: Use the "Multiple items" checkbox when adding multiple products to a single purchase or sale.

Analytics Dashboard

The Analytics Dashboard provides comprehensive insights into your business performance through interactive charts and visualizations. Each chart can be exported to Excel or Google Sheets for further analysis.

Geographic Analysis

  • Countries of Origin: Shows where your purchased products come from.
  • Countries of Destination: Shows where your products are being sold to, helping track your market reach.
  • Companies of Origin: Visualizes your supplier distribution - for instance, showing what percentage of products come from vendors.

Transaction Analysis

  • Total Transactions: Compares the number of purchases vs. sales over time.
  • Average Transaction Value: Tracks the average value of your purchases and sales, helping identify trends in spending and revenue.
  • Average Shipping Costs: Monitors shipping expenses over time for both purchases and sales (excluding transactions with free shipping).

Financial Performance

  • Total Expenses vs. Sales: Compares your total expenses against sales revenue, providing a clear view of business volume.
  • Distribution of Expenses: Breaks down expenses by category (e.g., equipment, supplies, shipping, taxes).
  • Distribution of Revenue: Shows how your revenue is distributed across different product categories and services.

Growth Metrics

  • Growth: Shows the percentage growth of both expenses and revenue over time, helping identify trends and seasonal patterns.
  • Total Profits: Tracks your profit margins by comparing revenue against expenses.

Management Insights

  • Transactions by Accountant: Shows the distribution of transactions handled by each accountant, helping monitor workload distribution.
Tip: You can customize the time range for all charts using the date range selector. Click the line/bar toggle to switch between visualization styles for applicable charts.
Note: Right-click any chart to access additional options including exporting to Microsoft Excel or Google Sheets.

Receipt Management

Keep your records organized by attaching and managing digital receipts for all transactions.

Adding Receipts

  1. When adding a purchase or sale, click the "Add Receipt" button
  2. Select the receipt file from your computer
  3. The receipt will be copied to your company's secure storage

To add receipts to existing transactions, right-click the transaction, select "Modify", and click the "Add receipt" button.

Exporting Receipts from the main screen

  1. Select the transactions you want to export receipts for
  2. Right-click and select "Export receipts"
  3. Choose a destination folder
  4. For multiple receipts, they will be organized in a dated folder

Exporting Receipts from the Receipt Manager

  1. Click the file button on the top left, then click "Export Receipts"
  2. Optionally filter the receipts you want to export
  3. Click the "Select all" button or press "Ctrl+A"
  4. Click the "Export" button and choose the destination

Spreadsheet Import/Export

Easily move your data in and out of Argo Sales Tracker using Excel spreadsheets.

Exporting Data

  1. Click File > Export
  2. Select "Excel spreadsheet (.xlsx) from the drop down menu"
  3. Choose a location for your export
  4. The exported file will contain separate worksheets for: Purchases, Sales, Categories, Products, Companies, and Accountants

Importing Data

  1. Prepare your spreadsheet following our template format
  2. Click File > Import > Excel spreadsheet
  3. Select your file and choose what to import
  4. Review and confirm the import
Important: When importing data, ensure your spreadsheet follows the exact format of our template. Download our template here.

Database Encryption

Argo Sales Tracker uses AES-256 encryption to protect your business data.

Security Features

  • Military-grade Encryption: All data is encrypted using AES-256, the same standard used by banks and military organizations
  • Secure Storage: Receipts and attachments are stored in an encrypted format
  • Backup Encryption: All backups are automatically encrypted
Note: Encryption is automatic and requires no additional setup from users.

Password Protection

Secure access to your business data with robust password protection and Windows Hello integration (paid version).

Setting Up Password Protection

  1. Go to Account > Settings > Security
  2. Click "Enable Password Protection"
  3. Create a strong password

Windows Hello Integration (Paid Version)

  • Use fingerprint, facial recognition, or a PIN for quick access
  • Maintains security while improving convenience
  • Works with any Windows Hello compatible device
Important: Store your password securely. If you forget it, your data cannot be recovered and will be lost forever! (A long time)

Regular Backups

It's crucial to regularly back up your business data to prevent any potential loss. We recommend making backups at least weekly, or after entering significant amounts of new data.

Creating a Backup

  1. Click File > Export as...
  2. Select "ArgoSales (.zip)" from the drop-down menu
  3. Choose a location for your backup
  4. Store backups in a secure location, preferably on a different device
Important: Regular backups are your safeguard against data loss due to hardware failure, accidents, or other unforeseen circumstances. Make it a habit to back up your data frequently!
